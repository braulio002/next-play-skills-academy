About Us

Next Play Skills Academy is a youth basketball program dedicated to helping athletes develop skills that will help them both on and off the court. Whether it's local development or national competitive play, Next Play offers both. This allows for a more balanced environment where your child's needs are met and they are given opportunities to be put in a position where their skill sets can be challenged for growth.
